Laser Rifle Build


This was the linchpin of my Fallout 76 GenCon Cosplay in 2019.

I modeled it in Fusion 360 - for 3d printing - from scratch using references from Fallout 4 and deduced measurements from official merchandise, this was an incredibly trying process on a number of fronts, not the least of which was how very many iterations I went through both digitally in Fusion and physically in 3d printed parts first getting the relative sizing right and then dialing in fit and accuracy of the printed parts.

After indivdually modelling every distinct part I merged components together as much as the printing and eventual painting processess would allow, this encompassed several weeks of trial and error.

Keypad
